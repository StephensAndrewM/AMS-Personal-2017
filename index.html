About

I love problem-solving and thinking creatively.

In 2016 I graduated from Tufts University with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

I currently live in the San Francisco Bay Area and work at Google on the G Suite platform backend.

I have experience in full-stack web engineering, front-end web design, data rollup pipelines, and Android app development.

I led a re-design of the print edition of The Tufts Daily, and wrote several internal tools to improve the production process.

I have worked on over 10 theatrical productions as lead sound designer, and many others as a sound board operator or sound engineer.

Outside of work I enjoy biking, baking gluten-free treats, and building great civilizations.
